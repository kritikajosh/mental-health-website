Severe Anxiety: Symptoms & Urgent Treatment

Common Symptoms of Severe Anxiety:

  • Intense and persistent worrying
  • Panic attacks
  • Feeling of impending doom
  • Extreme fear or dread
  • Physical symptoms such as rapid heartbeat, sweating, trembling, or shortness of breath
  • Avoidance of triggers or situations that cause anxiety
