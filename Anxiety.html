Anxiety Test (Self-Assessment)

Below is a list of questions designed for people who are experiencing anxiety-inducing thoughts. The questions relate to life experiences common among people who have been diagnosed with generalized anxiety disorder (GAD). Please read each question carefully, and indicate how often you have experienced the same or similar challenges in the past few months.

1.Do you worry about lots of different things? *






2.Do you have trouble controlling your worries? *






3.Do you get irritable and/or easily annoyed when anxious? *






4.Does worry or anxiety make you feel fatigued or worn out? *






5.Does worry or anxiety imterfere with falling and/or staying asleep? *






6.Does worry or anxiety make it hard to concentrate? *






7.Do you feel jumpy? *






8.Do you worry about how well you do things? *






9.Do you worry about things working out in the future? *
