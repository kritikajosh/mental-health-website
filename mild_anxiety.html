Mild Anxiety: Symptoms & Treatment

Common Symptoms of Mild Anxiety:

  • Excessive worrying
  • Restlessness or feeling on edge
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Irritability
  • Muscle tension
  • Difficulty falling or staying asleep

Treatment Methods for Managing Mild Anxiety:

  • Deep breathing exercises and relaxation techniques
  • Regular physical activity or exercise
  • Maintaining a healthy diet and limiting caffeine intake
  • Practicing mindfulness or meditation
  • Seeking support from friends, family, or a therapist
  • Consideration of professional therapy or counseling if symptoms persist or worsen
